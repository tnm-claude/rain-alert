Instructions

How to Use Rain Alert

Adding Locations:

  • Click "File" → "Add New Location"
  • Enter any address, city, or landmark
  • The system will geocode it automatically

Configuring Notifications:

  • Click "File" → "Settings"
  • Enable Slack, Telegram, or Email
  • Enter your webhook/token details
  • Click "Test Notifications" to verify

Understanding Alerts:

  • Red alerts = Active rain detected or approaching
  • Blue alerts = Snoozed via Slack (30 min)
  • System checks every 5 minutes
  • Predictive alerts with ETA and velocity
  • Alerts within 10km radius
  • Auto-expire after 30 minutes

Radar Map:

  • Shows last 2 hours of rain data
  • Use Play/Pause to animate
  • Zoom and pan the map freely
  • Data from RainViewer API
